Want to book us or say hi? Send your name and message below. Responses go to a spreadsheet.

Google Form embed

Create a Google Form with two fields: Name and Message. Turn on “Collect email addresses” in Settings if you want. Then in the form, click Send → the </> icon to get the embed code.

Paste the iframe code into this file (contact.html) where it says “Replace the placeholder below”. The form responses will save to your Google Sheet automatically.
